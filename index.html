Residential Solar

Our mission is to accelerate the adoption of solar energy by making it accessible, affordable, and hassle-free for homeowners. We believe that solar power has the potential to revolutionize the way we generate electricity, and we are committed to empowering individuals to take control of their energy consumption and contribute to a greener future.

Grid-tied Solar System

This is the most common type of residential solar system. It is connected to the electrical grid.

Photovoltaic Systems

PV systems are the most common type of residential solar installation.

Hybrid Solar System

A hybrid system combines solar panels with battery storage.

Community Solar

Community solar allows multiple homeowners to collectively benefit from a shared solar energy system.
